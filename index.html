✨

Proactive insights, not reports

Count2 tells you what matters before you go looking. Revenue trending down, AI costs spiking, invoices overdue — you get a tap on the shoulder, not a spreadsheet.

📈

Earnings — plain and simple

Money in, costs out, what's left. Accrual accounting under the hood so your numbers are correct, but you'll never see the word "debit" anywhere.

💧

Cash runway, always visible

The one number every founder actually obsesses over. Updated in real-time from your bank, projected forward 12 months automatically.

🔮

Rolling 12-month forecast, no spreadsheets

Count2 builds your forecast from your own spending patterns. Two optional questions. Zero spreadsheets. Confidence bands widen the further out you look — because honest uncertainty beats false precision.

🏦

Real bank data via Plaid

Connect your bank in 60 seconds. Transactions import automatically and Claude categorises them — AWS goes to Infrastructure, not "Uncategorised."

Equity & loans handled correctly

A funding round is not revenue. A loan repayment is not an expense. Count2 knows the difference and posts the right entries automatically.

📄

Invoice capture that works

Drop a PDF. Claude reads it, pulls out the amount, vendor, and date, and posts the journal entries. Or connect Stripe and invoices sync themselves.

⚖️

Balance sheet on demand

Not a daily tab — a download for when your investor asks. Always accurate because every transaction posts double-entry entries in the background.
